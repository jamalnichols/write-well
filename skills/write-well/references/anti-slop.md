# Anti-Slop

## Goal

Remove writing that sounds generated, inflated, managerial, or emotionally counterfeit.

## Lexical slop

Prefer cutting or replacing phrases like:

- robust
- seamless
- powerful
- leverage
- transformative
- at this point in time
- in order to
- it is important to note
- not only X but also Y when it adds no real force
- provides users with the ability to
- enables stakeholders to
- facilitates the process of

### Better pattern

- say what happened
- name who did it
- name what changed
- use a shorter word when it is equally precise

## Structural slop

Look for:

- buried actions hidden in nouns
- long openings with no clear subject
- sentence endings that trail off into weak qualifiers
- paragraphs that keep restarting instead of advancing
- transitions that announce movement without creating it

### Better pattern

- actors in subjects
- actions in verbs
- familiar information first
- new information last
- paragraph-level momentum

## Tonal slop

Remove:

- fake seriousness
- fake empathy
- fake certainty
- LinkedIn uplift
- thought-leadership cadence
- TED-talk vagueness
- faux “professionalism” that strips out human texture

### Warning signs

- abstract praise with no evidence
- motivational tone where the prose needs judgment
- inflated calmness around weak ideas
- obvious content-marketing energy

## Rhetorical slop

Reject:

- passive constructions used to hide agency
- euphemisms that blur responsibility
- grand summaries that only restate the intro
- “professional” abstractions covering thin thinking

## Before / after patterns

### Bad

It is important to note that our platform provides users with the ability to seamlessly leverage a robust workflow.

### Better

The platform gives users a simpler workflow.

### Better when specifics exist

The platform cuts the review process from five steps to two.

## Final check

Before returning the rewrite, ask:

- Does any sentence sound like generic product copy?
- Did I add confidence where the original had nuance?
- Did I replace a distinctive phrase with a safe but dull one?
- Did I improve the prose, or just smooth it into AI paste?
